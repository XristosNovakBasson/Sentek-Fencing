About

Company History:

Sentek was established in 2000 by Pieter Basson. From humble beginnings of a one-man enterprise the a multi personnel company it is today. Boasting a training section, where employees are trained to erect various types of fencing. We now boast 4 full time teams of 6 team members each, with lorries and approximately 75 contract employees, employed on an intermittent contract basis.

Strategy & Vision:

To be a beacon for positive change, embracing the challenges and opportunities of our times to advance understanding and change lives for the better. We will provide specialist knowledge, skills and learning experiences in the area of fencing to our clients. Through high-impact research and recognition as an international role player in areas of strategic importance regarding fencing security, we will deliver outcomes of significant value to our clients and staff loyalty, nationally and globally.

What We Do:

Sentek does erection of fencing. Sentek is also registered with the Department of Trade and Industry as an Electric Fence Compliance officer. Registration Number: Efsi 00605. All types of fencing are installed or erected by Sentek, including gate automation and the manufacturing of odd size palisade panels and burglar bars.
